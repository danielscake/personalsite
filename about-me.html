About Me

Hey, I’m Rugi.

I’m a design thinker. I like to think outside of the box and do my research. By doing that I won first place at the uXperience Design Camp 2.0 in October of 2016. Meeting people and learning what they are all about is important to me and to every project that I work on.

Secondly, I’m a designer. I design experiences for the web, video, and all things digital. I like to dab in designing real-world experiences. See ArtTech UX Design Project. I’ve been doing this for a couple years and I’ve learned a lot doing so.

Third I’m a filmmaker. Directing was a passion I had from an early age. I wanted to be YouTube star when YouTube was still young. I learned a lot doing that. I learned how to target people's emotions and create story arcs that push towards a goal. I’ve brought those experiences with me and design with emotion, empathy, and journeys in mind.

I used to think that I saw the world through a different lens - the lens of a camera.

I now see the world through the experience that I can create for people using digital tech.

Over the past year, I’ve learned a lot. I learned how to use Github, how to code my own website, and how to be a teaching assistant. But most of all I’ve learned how to apply the design thinking approach to every project that I work on. By doing so I’ve created some very comprehensive and full functioning ideas and projects.

A great design is one thing, but a great idea is a whole other world.
